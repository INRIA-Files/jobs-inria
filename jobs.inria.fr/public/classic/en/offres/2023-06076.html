2023-06076 - Two-years engineer position to work on the Kappa modeling platform.

Contract type : Fixed-term contract

Level of qualifications required : Graduate degree or equivalent

Fonction : Temporary scientific engineer

Level of experience : From 3 to 5 years

Context

* Kappa *

Kappa is a modeling environment dedicated to interacting agents.
Its development is primarily motivated by molecular systems biology.
It is made of user interfaces (web, apps), a stochastic simulation engine, and static and causal analysis tools. 
All Kappa software is open source and written in OCaml (interfaces are based on Ocsigen).

See also: https://kappalanguage.org

* EPI Antique *

The engineer will work in the team Antique (Inria Paris), hosted at École normale supérieure. 
The team Antique is conducting research on static analysis, and especially by the means of Abstract Interpretation.

Assignment

Assignments :
With the help of Jérôme Feret, the recruited person will be taken to maintain, document, and develop the Kappa modeling platform. 

For a better knowledge of the proposed research subject :
See also: https://kappalanguage.org

Collaboration :
The recruited person will be in connection with Jérôme Feret who is a research fellow at INRIA in the Antique project team. Jérôme Feret is the main developper of the Kappa modeling platform.

About Inria

Inria is the French national research institute dedicated to digital science and technology. It employs 2,600 people. Its 200 agile project teams, generally run jointly with academic partners, include more than 3,500 scientists and engineers working to meet the challenges of digital technology, often at the interface with other disciplines. The Institute also employs numerous talents in over forty different professions. 900 research support staff contribute to the preparation and development of scientific and entrepreneurial projects that have a worldwide impact.
