2023-05747 - Starting Research Position (SRP) - Tackling data interoperability in decentralised data marketplaces

Contract type : Fixed-term contract

Renewable contract : Oui

Level of qualifications required : PhD or equivalent

Fonction : Tempary Research Position

Context

The MIMOVE team at Inria Paris undertakes research enabling next-generation mobile distributed systems and networks, from their conception and design to their runtime support, with focus on both middleware and data. MIMOVE has longstanding expertise in mobile and service-oriented computing, semantic technologies, interoperability, system emergence and evolution, edge/fog computing, and mobile crowdsensing. MIMOVE works on these topics through a great number of national and international collaborations with academia and industry, which include large-scale software development of real-world systems. MIMOVE’s research results have an impact on various application domains; MIMOVE focuses in particular on the application areas of IoT, smart cities, citizen participation.

Inria MIMOVE is one of the 13 partners of the SEDIMARK project (https://sedimark.eu/) of the Horizon Europe Framework Programme. The EU has a vision to become the world’s largest and most secure Data Hub where businesses will be able to exploit data for promoting research, education, and business. This requires significant investments in the development of (among others) intelligent tools for data interoperability, secure data sharing, and data discovery. SEDIMARK aims to provide an enriched secure decentralised data and services marketplace, where scattered data from various domains and geographical locations within the EU can be easily generated, cleaned, protected, discovered, enriched with metadata, AI and analytics and exploited for diverse business and research scenarios.

Assignment

Inria MIMOVE's role in the SEDIMARK project is summarized as follows:

  • Bring in longstanding expertise in semantic technologies, in particular related to systems and the IoT;
  • Lead and contribute to the design of the SEDIMARK semantic models and related tools for enabling data interoperability across providers and domains.

Main activities

In the context identified above, the SRP Researcher will be the main contributor for Inria MIMOVE to the SEDIMARK project, with the following principal activities:

  • Lead the task that aims at designing and developing the SEDIMARK semantic models and related tools;
  • Contribute to other tasks, dealing with the interplay between semantic technologies and AI/ML based solutions for data management and data analytics;
  • Contribute to requirement elicitation for the overall architecture and to its design;
  • Contribute to the overall platform integration and evaluation;
  • Contribute to the project dissemination and exploitation representing Inria as an academic partner.

In addition, the SRP Researcher is expected to contribute to MIMOVE's research in relation to the above identified and other related topics, but also propose, initiate and lead new research topics. 




 

 

Skills

The candidate should have a PhD in Computer Science with expertise – including experience in the implementation of related software prototypes – in one and possibly several of the following topics:

  • Distributed systems,
  • Middleware architectures and protocols,
  • Internet of Things,
  • Semantic technologies,
  • Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning.

About Inria

Inria is the French national research institute dedicated to digital science and technology. It employs 2,600 people. Its 200 agile project teams, generally run jointly with academic partners, include more than 3,500 scientists and engineers working to meet the challenges of digital technology, often at the interface with other disciplines. The Institute also employs numerous talents in over forty different professions. 900 research support staff contribute to the preparation and development of scientific and entrepreneurial projects that have a worldwide impact.
